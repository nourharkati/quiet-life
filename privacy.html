Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you.

Effective May 25, 2026

Quiet Life products are designed to operate locally on your device. We do not build systems around surveillance, behavioral analytics, or advertising infrastructure.

Data Collection

Our applications are designed with a local-first architecture. We do not require accounts, cloud identities, or centralized user profiles.

No Accounts You can use our software without creating an account or providing personal information.
No Tracking We do not collect analytics, behavioral telemetry, advertising identifiers, or usage profiles.
Local Storage Your data remains stored locally on your device within the native operating system sandbox environment.

Notifications

If notifications are enabled, reminders are generated locally through native operating system frameworks. Notification schedules are not processed through external servers or remote infrastructure.

Third-Party Sharing

Because we do not collect personal data, we have no user information to sell, analyze, monetize, or share with third parties.

Contact

For questions regarding privacy or application architecture, contact us at support@quiet-life.org .
