Last Updated: May 2026

Privacy Policy

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or usage data.

All information, and notification schedules are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.

No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking frameworks, or cloud services are integrated into this app.

We do not require user accounts, permissions, or network access. Your data never leaves your device.

By using our apps, you agree that no data collection occurs.

Verdura App Guide

Start by browsing the Botanical Guide. tap any plant to see its full care profile. Tap the heart to add it to 'My Garden'. Choose your first watering time, then tap Activate Smart Cycle to set your reminder. When you water, simply tap the drop "💧" button next to the plant in My Garden. Verdura will instantly log it, update your history, and schedule the next prep alert exactly one day before your plant needs attention. That's it. No complex settings. Just calm, confident care, one drop at a time.

Common Questions

  • Where is my data stored?
    All data stays on your device. Nothing is collected or transmitted.
  • How do I reset my history?
    Delete and reinstall the app (history is local-only).
  • Why didn't I get a notification?
    Check Settings → Notifications → "Allow Notifications" is ON.
